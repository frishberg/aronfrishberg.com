About the Author

By Aron Frishberg · San Francisco

San Francisco, Calif., circa 2026.

Aron Frishberg is the CEO and co-founder of Gatsby Robotics, a San Francisco company sending humanoid robots to clean apartments. Before that, he was a student at the University of Chicago, double‑majoring in Computer Science and Economics (specialization in Business). He dropped out to build Gatsby.

He grew up in Scarsdale, New York, and now lives in San Francisco. Outside of work, he kayaks, makes homemade ice cream, and works with robots. He likes to create things, especially ones that don't fit neatly into a category, including this paper.

He built this website to share the things he makes, whether a mathematical theory, a dataset, a chess analysis, or a company. He hopes you find something worth reading.
