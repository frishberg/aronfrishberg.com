Just One A: The Podcast

Fifteen conversations with Forbes 30 Under 30 honorees, plus the President of Retail Banking at Bank of America.

By Aron Frishberg

During the finals week of my first semester at Brandeis, I had the idea to start a podcast where I interviewed people from the Forbes 30 Under 30 list and asked them about their experiences starting companies, and about their lives in general.

It was one of the hardest projects I've ever undertaken, including many, many unanswered emails, but within two months I had interviewed 12 people from the 2023 Forbes 30 Under 30, as well as Holly O'Neill, the President of Retail Banking at Bank of America. The official website, where you can listen on YouTube, Spotify, Apple Music, or Amazon Music, is here.
